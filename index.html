Our Celebration Guide వేడుక మార్గదర్శి

The Haldi is our joyful pre-wedding celebration with turmeric, flowers, music, and a lot of family energy. It is bright, playful, and one of the most personal parts of the weekend.

What to expect

  • Turmeric blessings from family and close friends
  • Music, laughter, and a little friendly family competition
  • Lots of yellow, marigolds, and photo moments
Wear something comfortable in yellow or orange, and do not worry about a little turmeric. For outfit ideas, see Attire.

Our wedding ceremony is a traditional Telugu Hindu ceremony led by a priest, with blessings, rituals, and a few playful moments woven in. Even if the prayers are unfamiliar, you will still be able to feel the meaning and joy in the room.

What to expect

  • Prayers, garlands, and family blessings
  • Signature Telugu moments like Talambralu and the seven steps
  • A mix of sacred rituals and lighthearted moments
During the ceremony, we would love for you to stay present and keep phones tucked away. For what to wear, see Attire.

The reception is our evening celebration with dinner, toasts, music, and a full dance floor. It will feel festive, welcoming, and a little bit like the best kind of family party.

What to expect

  • Cocktail hour, dinner, and family toasts
  • A mix of Indian and American wedding traditions
  • Bollywood, Telugu, and crowd-favorite dance music
Wear something festive that you can move in, and come ready to dance. More details are in Attire.

Attire దుస్తుల నియమాలు

Indian celebrations traditionally place great importance on attire as a reflection of culture and festivity. The most important thing is that you feel comfortable and enjoy yourself — please try your best within the guidelines, but don't stress. We're just excited to celebrate with you!

An intimate, joyous pre-wedding shower where turmeric and water are applied in a fun and messy ritual with games to signify purification. Yellow is considered auspicious — a color that brings happiness and positive energy, marking a fresh beginning for the couple.

Colors
Yellow, White & Orange

Outfit Inspiration

Women

Traditional Indian attire like a lehenga, sharara, chudidhar, or saree also works beautifully, and a summer semi-formal long dress is welcome too.

Men

Semi-formal cotton or linen shirts with trousers work well, and a kurta is a great fit for the celebration.

✦

Traditionally Indian weddings have multiple outfit changes. We will be changing outfits from wedding to reception, and everyone is encouraged and welcome to do the same.

Outfit Inspiration

Women

Traditional Indian attire or formal floor-length gowns are both perfect for the ceremony and reception.

Men

Formal suits or sherwanis are both a great fit for the wedding and reception.
